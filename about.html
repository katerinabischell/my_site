Who I Am

I am an environmental scientist and data analyst with a passion for water resources, watershed management, pollution remediation, and environmental data science. My work sits at the intersection of fieldwork, research, and technology — from analyzing California water quality to designing watershed management plans and developing pollution remediation strategies.

Whether collecting samples in the field, building interactive data dashboards in R, or writing technical reports, I bring scientific rigor and genuine curiosity to every project. I believe that clear communication of environmental data is as critical as the science itself.
