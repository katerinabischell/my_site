Who I Am

I am an environmental scientist with an M.E.S.M. from the UC Santa Barbara Bren School — one of the world's top environmental graduate programs. My work sits at the intersection of fieldwork, data science, and policy: from training AI-powered pollinator monitoring systems, to analyzing California coastal water quality, to designing pollution remediation strategies for real-world watersheds.

I recently joined Applied Marine Sciences as an Environmental Scientist, where I bring a combination of field research experience, quantitative modeling skills, and scientific communication ability to marine and water resource challenges.
